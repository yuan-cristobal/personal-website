Tell Me Where to Put the Anger

moving out,

everything is slipping from my hands and im excited for it.
i feel like I’ve been mourning this city for half
a decade now, and i feel that the end is near.

i’ve known every crack and hidden places of this city,
every restaurant and malls, every street lights and pavements.
i want to go for a while now and when i go,
i know i will leave clawmarks on it.

but i’m taking every part of me with me,
the 11 years old me who cried on his birthday,
the 15 years old me who was forced to hide himself,
and the 18 years old me who found peace and clarity.

i will forever hold this city close to my heart but its time to go now.
